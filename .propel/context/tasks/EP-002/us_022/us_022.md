# User Story - US_022

## Story ID
* US_022

## Story Title
* No-show risk score calculation at booking time

## Description
* As a Staff member, I want each new appointment to be assigned a no-show risk score at booking time using rule-based logic, so that I can proactively identify and outreach high-risk patients before the appointment date.

## Acceptance Criteria

### AC-001: Risk score is calculated and stored on every new appointment
- **Given**: A patient books an appointment via `POST /appointments`
- **When**: The appointment is created
- **Then**: The `noShowRiskScore` field on the Appointment entity is set to an integer between 0 and 100; the score is derived from at least: prior no-show count, booking lead time in days, and intake completion status for previous appointments

### AC-002: High no-show history increases risk score
- **Given**: A patient has 3 or more prior no-show appointments
- **When**: A new appointment is booked
- **Then**: The `noShowRiskScore` is ≥ 70 (high-risk threshold); the appointment appears in the Staff high-risk filter

### AC-003: Long lead time decreases risk score contribution
- **Given**: A patient books an appointment 14 or more days in advance
- **When**: The risk score is calculated
- **Then**: The lead-time component contributes a lower risk factor than a booking made 1 day in advance

### AC-004: Staff can view the no-show risk score on the daily schedule
- **Given**: Appointments for today are loaded on the Staff schedule
- **When**: `GET /schedule/today` is called by Staff
- **Then**: Each appointment entry includes `noShowRiskScore` and a `riskFlag` boolean (`true` if score ≥ configured threshold, default 70)

### AC-005: Risk score is recalculated only at booking time, not retrospectively
- **Given**: A patient's historical no-show count increases after an appointment is already booked
- **When**: The existing appointment's record is read
- **Then**: The `noShowRiskScore` on the existing appointment is unchanged; only newly booked appointments receive a fresh score calculation

## Edge Cases
* First-time patient with no history — score defaults to a baseline moderate-risk value (e.g., 30) representing unknown history; not zero (unknown ≠ low risk)
* Intake completion status unavailable at booking time — the intake component of the score defaults to "incomplete" (higher risk contribution) until intake is submitted
* Score threshold is configurable — read from `AppSettings.NoShowRiskThreshold`; changing the threshold affects the `riskFlag` display but does not retroactively recalculate stored scores
