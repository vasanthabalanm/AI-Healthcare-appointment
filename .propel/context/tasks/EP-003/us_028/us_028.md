# User Story - US_028

## Story ID
* US_028

## Story Title
* Automated email appointment reminders with cancellation link

## Description
* As a patient, I want to receive reminder emails at 48 hours and 2 hours before my appointment containing a cancellation or reschedule link, so that I can easily take action if I can no longer attend.

## Acceptance Criteria

### AC-001: T-48h email reminder is sent and includes a cancellation link
- **Given**: The T-48h Hangfire email reminder job fires
- **When**: The email is dispatched via MailKit
- **Then**: The patient receives an email containing the appointment date, time, location; a direct cancellation link `https://{host}/appointments/{id}/cancel?token={secureToken}`; and a reschedule link; the link token is single-use and expires after 48 hours

### AC-002: T-2h email reminder is sent with same content structure
- **Given**: The T-2h Hangfire email reminder job fires
- **When**: The email is dispatched
- **Then**: The patient receives the 2-hour reminder email with appointment details and an active cancellation link

### AC-003: Reminder email is sent over SMTPS or STARTTLS
- **Given**: MailKit is configured for the reminder email job
- **When**: The SMTP connection is established
- **Then**: The connection uses SMTPS (port 465) or STARTTLS (port 587); no plain-text SMTP connection is used; SMTP credentials are sourced from environment variables

### AC-004: Cancelled appointment prevents pending email reminders from firing
- **Given**: A patient cancels an appointment with > 48 hours remaining
- **When**: The cancellation is processed
- **Then**: Both T-48h and T-2h Hangfire email reminder jobs are deleted; neither email is sent after cancellation

## Edge Cases
* Cancellation link clicked after appointment time — token validation rejects the link with "Appointment time has passed"; HTTP 400 returned
* Reminder email delivery fails all 3 retries — dead-letter queue entry; Staff dashboard alert; patient is not notified of the delivery failure to avoid confusion
* Reminder template content update — template changes are deployed via code; in-flight scheduled jobs use the template version at execution time (latest deployed template)
